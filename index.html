Application
The entire application process, from obtaining initial information to applying for your student visa, should begin 3 to 6 months before you wish to start studying in the United States.

Steps:

1. Reach out to us via email or phone call for an informational consultation

2. Schedule phone interview with us so we can get to know the student and parents

3. Send agreement via email and after review, sign our contract and pay 60% of the fees

4. We will provide you with detailed instructions and forms to complete so we have your information

5. We will help fill out all applications (i.e., highschool or college forms) on your behalf

6. We will help you submit all applications. Once approved, we will help you apply for the I-20 visa once you pay the remaining 40% of fees

7. We will provide you with visa interview guidance (i.e., how to prepare, what to study for, etc.)

8. After you get your visa, we can help arrange for transportation (i.e., plane) and pick you up from the LAX airport
